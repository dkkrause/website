Professional Summary

I am a Software Engineering Executive who forms highly motivated teams that are excited about their work and proud of their accomplishments. I lead these teams to deliver software that exceeds customer expectations in both functionality and quality in the face of conflicting priorities and demands. I contribute to deep technical product architecture, design and implementation discussions. I communicate effectively with employees, peers, and customers. I meet budgets and schedules. I balance strategy with tactics.

I graduated from U. C. Berkeley with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I started as a software engineer, however I found that my greatest skills and accomplishments were in technical engineering management. I have worked for Harris Corporation and Lucent Technologies, as part of the acquisition of Ascend Communications. Most recently, I held the role of Vice President, Certified Platforms Engineering at Wind River, which was acquired by Intel in 2010.

My management skills are well-rounded and come with extensive experience. They include:

  • Team Building
  • Motivation
  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Distributed/Offshore Teams
  • Scheduling and Budgeting
  • Customer Interface
  • Quality and Process

My technical abilities are also deep and include:

  • Networking

  • Safety Systems
  • Industrial and Medical
  • Certification (DO-178B, IEC 61508, ISO 9001, AS9100, CMMI, etc.)
